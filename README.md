**Arcana** is an immersive and private environment designed for developers and Prompt Engineers who want to organize their work (snippets, logic, and prompts) with absolute security. Unlike other tools, Arcana is **100% Offline-First**, ensuring your data never leaves your device.

---

## üåå The Aesthetic: Mystic-Tech

* **Immersive Interface**: Mist effects, pulsing stars, and subtle transparencies.
* **Deck System**: Your notes are organized into modular cards with **flip** animations and fluid interaction.
* **Relaxing UI**: The app's design was crafted for those who work late into the night. The colors were carefully chosen to avoid eye strain!
